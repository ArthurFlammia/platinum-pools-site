# MP1 Proposal — Platinum Pools

## Client Brief

**Client:** Platinum Pools (Vernon, owner) — owner-operated pool service
company at 16 Central St, Rowley, MA

**Purpose:** A clean, phone-first site that gets homeowners to call,
with clearer service descriptions than the current site.

**Audience:** Homeowners with pools on the Massachusetts North Shore,
usually searching on their phones. Their top questions: do you service
my town, what does weekly service include, and when should I schedule
openings and closings.

**Key action:** Call the business. The owner prefers phone contact, so
the number is a tappable button in the header of every page.

**Pages/sections needed:**
- Home (hero, about, services teaser, service area)
- Services (weekly service detail, seasonal/repair services, gallery
  once photos arrive)
- Contact (phone-first, address, service area)

**Content status:** Text content is in place from the interview. Still
needed from the client: photos of pool openings, repairs, and completed
pools for the gallery section (currently commented out in the code so
nothing deploys broken).

**Style preferences:** Blue, white, and gray to fit the brand. Clean and
professional — nothing flashy or complicated.

**Inspiration sites:** The owner described what he likes rather than
naming specific sites: services and service area visible immediately,
customer reviews, and a clear call-to-action button. The design follows
that pattern.
